# 🦀 rusty-no-more

i got tired of saying "i should learn rust"
so here we are.

---

## what this is

this repo is basically me learning rust in public.

no grand plan.
no structured course.
just:

* me trying things
* breaking things
* getting bullied by the compiler
* and slowly… getting it

---

## why i'm doing this

i like understanding things *properly*. not just "it works".

rust feels like one of those languages where:

* you can't fake it
* you either understand it… or it humbles you

so instead of half-learning it, i want to go all in and build a real mental model.

also — writing things down helps me think better.

---

## what you'll find here

honestly, a mix of everything:

* notes in *my* words (not textbook rust)
* small experiments
* dumb mistakes (documented proudly)
* things that finally clicked after 2 hours of confusion
* random mini projects as i go

this is not a tutorial repo.
it's more like a thinking log.

---

## how i'm approaching this

* if i can't explain it simply, i don't understand it
* if something feels magical, i dig deeper
* consistency > motivation
* build small things, not just read

---

## goal (not strict, but yeah)

i want to reach a point where:

* i can build real systems in rust
* the borrow checker feels like a guide, not an enemy
* and i don't have to "translate" from another language in my head
